Action Priority: Low = Green, Medium = Amber, High = Red.
Red – High Risk (12 to 25): Stop and review immediately.
Amber – Medium Risk (6 to 10): Additional controls required before proceeding.
Green – Low Risk (1 to 5): Broadly acceptable with normal controls.
